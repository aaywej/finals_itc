Pricing FAQ

Q: How do you calculate rush fees?

A: Rush adds 30% of the total order cost for priority completion.

Q: Can I cancel or change my order?

A: Minor changes are allowed before work starts. Cancelation policies apply after payment.

Q: Do you offer discounts for multiple orders?

A: Yes! Bundles like 3-pose packs or repeat clients may receive discounts.
