FAQ

Q: Are prices fixed?

A: Base prices are not fixed depending on how many pages or the complexity the site needs, add-ons can change total cost.

Q: Do you offer discounts?

A: Discounts are set during special occasions (e.g Christmas, Valentines, etc).

Q: Does it have a maximum of pages?

A: The maximum goes up to only 5 pages, the user will have to provide the links itself.

Q: Do you do moodboard rentries?

A: Yes I do, however I need references on what kind of rentry the user would prefer to have it look like.

Q: How long do commissions usually take?

A: Usually 3–10 business days depending on the complexity.

Q:How many revisions can I request?

A: 2 minor revisions are included with every commission.

Q: What is the payment?

A: Payments are through GCash, Paypal, or VGen. It is a 50/50, 50% upfront and 50% after.
