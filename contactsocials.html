Frequently Asked Questions

How long until you respond?
Usually within 24 hours! If I'm busy, it might take a little longer, but I always reply.
Can I ask about commissions through DMs?
Absolutely! DM me anytime for inquiries or price checks before ordering.
Where do I place a commission?
Message me directly on socials or fill out the commission form on this site.
Will I get progress updates?
Yes! I send work-in-progress shots (WIPs) so you can review before finalizing.
Can I request changes afterward?
Small fixes are fine, but major changes may incur additional costs.
Do you take rush commissions?
Yes, depending on availability. Rush fees may apply.
What's your refund policy?
Full refunds only if work hasn't started. Partial refunds apply once progress has begun.
