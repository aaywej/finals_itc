FAQ

Q: How long until you respond?

A: Usually within 24 hours! If I’m busy, it might take a little longer, but I’ll always get back to you.

Q: Can I ask about commissions through DMs?

A: Absolutely! Feel free to DM me anytime for inquiries or price checks before ordering.

Q: Where do I place a commission?

A: You can message me directly on my socials or fill out the commission form in this website.

Q: Will I get updates while you work on it?

A: Yes! I send WIPs (work-in-progress shots) through DMs so you can check and approve before finalizing.

Q: Do you post commissions on your page?

A: Sometimes! I usually ask permission first. You can request to keep it private if you’d like.

Q: Can I ask for changes after it’s done?

A: Small fixes are fine, but major changes after completion may cost extra.

Q: Do you take rush commissions?

A: Yes, depending on my schedule! Rush fees may apply for tight deadlines.

Q: What’s your refund policy?

A: Full refunds are only possible if I haven’t started yet. Once progress has begun, only partial refunds apply.
